# ISO 27001 Certification

Kubidu is ISO 27001 certified, demonstrating our commitment to information security management.

## What is ISO 27001?

ISO 27001 is the international standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS). It provides a framework for:

- Identifying security risks
- Implementing controls
- Continuously improving security
- Managing security incidents

## FAQ

### Does this make my app ISO 27001 compliant?

No. Your app and processes are separate. However, using Kubidu simplifies your path to certification by handling infrastructure security.

### How often are you audited?

- Annual surveillance audits
- Full recertification every 3 years
- Continuous internal audits

### Can I audit Kubidu directly?

Enterprise customers can arrange on-site audits. Contact your account manager.

### What happens if you fail an audit?

We'd work to remediate findings immediately. Significant failures would be communicated to customers with an action plan.
